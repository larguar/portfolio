SKMac

SKMac is a manufacturer’s representative firm serving professional audio, video, lighting, MI, and security markets.

Since 1926, the sales experts at SKMac have been matching each of their manufacturer’s products with companies in need of AV.

Their slogan is “make waves” and they’re a goofy, fun group of people so I wanted to design a website that mimicked those qualities. I designed and built out the responsive SKMac website in WordPress with key elements including the Homepage hero section’s interactive Javascript wave, the Homepage logos that change on page refresh using JSON (in order to include all of their manufacturers), and the playful headshot hover effects on the Our People page.
