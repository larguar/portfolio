A little more about me

My love for digital design bloomed when I created my first Myspace page in 2007 and discovered the wonderful world of code. The dev life allows for the perfect balance of creative and technical, while my background in design forces subconsciously detailed outcomes.

My four years at MICA taught me the value of taking unconventional design approaches to solve problems and the importance of being open minded about criticism. I’m a huge typography nerd and I’m more likely to trust a company with a nice looking site.
